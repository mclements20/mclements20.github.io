How to Play Magic: The Gathering

Magic: The Gathering is a complex and strategic collectible card game. Here are the basic steps to get started:

  1. Shuffle your deck of cards.
  2. Draw 7 cards as your starting hand.
  3. Decide who goes first (usually done by a coin flip).
  4. Start your turn:

Step 1: Shuffle your deck thoroughly to ensure a random distribution of cards.

Step 2: Draw 7 cards from your shuffled deck. This forms your starting hand.

Step 3: Decide who goes first, often done by a coin flip or mutual agreement.

Step 4: Begin your turn by untapping your permanents (if any), drawing a card, and then proceeding to your main phase where you can play land cards and cast spells.

These are just the very basics of how to play Magic. The game involves many more rules, card types, and strategies. To learn more, you can refer to the official Magic: The Gathering "How to Play" guide.
